# The Accident Guides

The Accident Guides is a static, educational publishing platform that provides
general information about accidents, injuries, and related topics.

This site is designed to help readers understand common concepts, timelines,
and considerations following accidents — **not** to provide legal or medical advice.

---

## What This Site Is

- An **educational content platform**
- A **local guide and research directory**
- A **static website built for clarity, compliance, and accessibility**

---

## What This Site Is Not

- ❌ Not a law firm
- ❌ Not a medical provider
- ❌ Not a referral service
- ❌ Not lead generation by referral fee
- ❌ Not a source of legal or medical advice

---

## Content Structure

### Global Guides (`/guides/`)
Educational, non-city-specific content covering:
- Accident checklists
- General timelines
- Research considerations
- Common post-accident questions

These pages are informational only and apply broadly.

### City Guides (`/phoenix/`)
City-specific educational context, including:
- Accident categories
- Local considerations
- Research-oriented firm directories

City pages link up to global guides for general education.

3. Build command: _none_
4. Output directory: `/`
5. Environment: Static
6. Auto-deploy on push
