About Slip and Fall Accidents in Phoenix

Slip and fall injuries can occur in many settings, including grocery stores, shopping centers, parking lots, apartment complexes, and public buildings.

In Phoenix, extreme heat can affect pavement, flooring materials, and outdoor surfaces, sometimes increasing the risk of slips or falls.

Common Causes of Slip and Fall Accidents

  • Wet or uneven flooring
  • Cracked sidewalks or pavement
  • Poor lighting
  • Loose rugs or mats
  • Obstructed walkways

What To Do After a Slip and Fall

  1. Seek medical attention if you are injured
  2. Report the incident to the property owner or manager
  3. Take photos of the area and any hazards
  4. Note the date, time, and location of the fall
  5. Collect contact information from any witnesses
  6. Ask for an incident report if one is available
  7. Keep copies of all related documents
