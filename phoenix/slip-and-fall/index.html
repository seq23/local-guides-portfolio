Understanding Slip and Fall Accidents

Slip and fall accidents can occur in a wide range of locations, including retail stores, apartment complexes, office buildings, sidewalks, and public spaces. Factors such as surface conditions, lighting, and maintenance practices may influence how these incidents happen.

In Phoenix, slip and fall incidents may occur both indoors and outdoors, with conditions influenced by weather, construction activity, and property use. Each situation is unique and may involve different considerations.
