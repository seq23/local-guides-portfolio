Accident Evidence Checklist (Phoenix)

After an accident, people often focus on medical care and safety first. When possible, gathering basic documentation can help preserve details about what occurred.

This checklist is provided for general educational purposes only. It does not constitute legal advice.

Scene Documentation

  • Photos or videos of vehicles, damage, and surroundings
  • Images showing weather, lighting, or road conditions
  • Location details such as street names or landmarks

People Involved

  • Names and contact information of involved parties
  • Insurance information, if applicable
  • Names and contact details of witnesses

Official Records

  • Police or incident reports, if one was created
  • Incident numbers or reference IDs
  • Employer or property reports, when applicable

Medical and Expense Records

  • Medical visit summaries or discharge paperwork
  • Receipts for related expenses
  • Notes about symptoms or limitations

General Reminders

  • Only gather information when it is safe to do so
  • Do not alter or modify original records
  • Every situation is different

This site does not provide legal or medical advice. For guidance specific to your situation, consult a qualified professional.
