About Pedestrian Accidents in Phoenix

Pedestrian accidents can occur at intersections, crosswalks, parking lots, and along busy roadways throughout Phoenix.

Factors such as high traffic volumes, wide roads, and limited shade or visibility can contribute to pedestrian safety risks in urban areas.

Common Factors in Pedestrian Accidents

  • Drivers failing to yield at crosswalks
  • Distracted driving
  • Poor visibility or lighting conditions
  • Speeding in pedestrian-heavy areas
  • Vehicles turning through intersections

What To Do After a Pedestrian Accident

  1. Call 911 immediately if you are injured
  2. Move to a safe location if possible
  3. Seek medical attention, even if injuries seem minor
  4. Document the scene with photos or videos
  5. Collect contact information from witnesses
  6. Obtain a copy of any police or incident report
  7. Keep copies of all related records
