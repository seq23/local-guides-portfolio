Rideshare Accidents in Phoenix

This page provides general educational information about rideshare accidents in Phoenix, Arizona. Rideshare accidents may involve drivers using platforms such as Uber or Lyft and can raise unique insurance and coverage questions.

Understanding Rideshare Accidents

Rideshare accidents may involve multiple parties, including the rideshare driver, passengers, other motorists, or pedestrians. Insurance coverage can vary depending on the driver’s status at the time of the accident.

When researching rideshare accidents, individuals often look for information about platform insurance policies, accident reporting procedures, and how claims are generally evaluated.
