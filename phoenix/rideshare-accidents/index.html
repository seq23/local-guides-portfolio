About Rideshare Accidents in Phoenix

Rideshare accidents may involve vehicles operated through app-based services, including situations where a passenger, another driver, a cyclist, or a pedestrian is injured.

These accidents can occur during pick-ups, drop-offs, or while a rideshare driver is actively transporting a passenger.

Common Rideshare Accident Scenarios

  • Collisions during passenger pick-up or drop-off
  • Accidents while a rideshare driver is en route to a request
  • Crashes involving multiple vehicles
  • Incidents involving pedestrians or cyclists

What To Do After a Rideshare Accident

  1. Call 911 if anyone is injured
  2. Move to a safe location if possible
  3. Seek medical attention promptly
  4. Document the scene and vehicle information
  5. Collect contact information from all parties involved
  6. Keep copies of any incident or police reports
