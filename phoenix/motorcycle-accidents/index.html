About Motorcycle Accidents in Phoenix

Motorcyclists face increased risk on Phoenix roadways due to high traffic volumes, high-speed roads, and limited physical protection compared to passenger vehicles.

Motorcycle accidents may occur on highways, surface streets, intersections, and neighborhood roads throughout the Phoenix area.

Common Factors in Motorcycle Accidents

  • Drivers failing to see motorcycles
  • Left-turn collisions at intersections
  • Unsafe lane changes
  • Road debris or uneven pavement
  • Speed or sudden stops

What To Do After a Motorcycle Accident

  1. Call 911 if you are injured or in danger
  2. Move to a safe location if possible
  3. Seek medical care promptly
  4. Document the scene with photos or videos
  5. Exchange contact and insurance information
  6. Avoid discussing fault at the scene
  7. Keep copies of all accident-related documents
