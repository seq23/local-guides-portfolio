About Truck Accidents in Phoenix

Truck accidents often involve larger vehicles and can result in more serious injuries. These crashes may include commercial trucks, delivery vehicles, or other large commercial carriers operating throughout the Phoenix area.

Many truck accidents in Phoenix occur on major routes such as I-10, I-17, and Loop 101, where commercial traffic is common.

Why Truck Accidents Are Different

  • Commercial insurance policies may apply
  • Federal and state safety rules can be involved
  • Multiple parties may be connected to the vehicle or driver

These factors can make truck accidents more complex than typical passenger vehicle crashes.

What To Do After a Truck Accident

  1. Call 911 if anyone is injured
  2. Move to safety if possible
  3. Seek medical attention promptly
  4. Document the scene with photos or videos
  5. Collect contact and insurance information
  6. Avoid discussing fault at the scene
  7. Keep copies of all accident-related records
