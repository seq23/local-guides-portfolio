Understanding Car Accidents in Phoenix

Phoenix is a large metropolitan area with extensive freeway systems, high daily commuter traffic, and frequent road construction. Collisions may occur in a wide range of circumstances, including highway travel, surface streets, and residential areas.

Factors such as traffic volume, road design, weather conditions, and time of day can influence how accidents occur and what information may be relevant when researching next steps.
