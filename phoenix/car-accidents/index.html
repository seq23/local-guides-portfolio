About Car Accidents in Phoenix

Car accidents are common in Phoenix due to highways, commuter traffic, and year-round driving conditions.

Accidents can happen on surface streets, freeways, parking lots, and residential roads throughout the city and surrounding areas.

What To Do After a Car Accident

  1. Call 911 if anyone is hurt
  2. Move to a safe location if possible
