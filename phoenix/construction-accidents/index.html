About Construction Accidents in Phoenix

Construction accidents can occur on commercial job sites, residential developments, road projects, and infrastructure work throughout the Phoenix area.

Workers, pedestrians, and drivers may be affected by construction-related hazards, especially in areas with ongoing development or roadway improvements.

Common Construction Accident Hazards

  • Falls from heights or scaffolding
  • Falling objects or debris
  • Equipment or machinery incidents
  • Trench or excavation hazards
  • Electrocution or utility-related incidents

What To Do After a Construction Accident

  1. Call 911 if there are serious injuries
  2. Move to a safe location
