About Bicycle Accidents in Phoenix

Bicycle accidents can occur on city streets, bike lanes, shared paths, and at intersections throughout Phoenix.

High traffic volumes, wide roadways, and limited protected bike infrastructure in some areas can increase risks for cyclists.

Common Factors in Bicycle Accidents

  • Drivers failing to see cyclists
  • Vehicles turning across bike lanes
  • Dooring incidents from parked cars
  • Road debris or uneven pavement
  • Speed differences between vehicles and bicycles

What To Do After a Bicycle Accident

  1. Call 911 if you are injured
  2. Move to a safe location if possible
  3. Seek medical attention promptly
  4. Document the scene with photos or videos
  5. Collect driver and witness contact information
  6. Obtain a copy of any police or incident report
  7. Keep copies of all related records
