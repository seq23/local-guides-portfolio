Understanding Bicycle Accidents

Bicycle accidents can occur in a variety of settings, including city streets, intersections, bike lanes, and shared-use paths. Factors such as traffic flow, road design, and visibility can play a role.

When researching bicycle accidents, individuals often seek information about right-of-way rules, helmet use, injury documentation, and how insurance claims may apply to cyclists.
