Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you visit The Accident Guides.

The Accident Guides is an independent educational publishing platform that provides general information related to accidents, injuries, and recovery topics. We also publish clearly labeled advertising content.

We are not a law firm, medical provider, or referral service, and we do not provide legal or medical advice.

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, we may collect limited technical information automatically, such as:

Information collected is used only for legitimate business and editorial purposes, including:
