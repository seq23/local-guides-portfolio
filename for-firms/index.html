Advertising & Sponsorships

The Accident Guides partners with a limited number of firms through clearly labeled, high-visibility sponsorships alongside educational accident content.

We do not sell leads, referrals, or outcomes. Sponsorships provide visibility, not endorsements.

Why Firms Advertise Here

  • High-intent readers actively researching next steps after an accident
  • Clean, calm environment — no pop-ups, no bait-and-switch
  • Fixed inventory and transparent pricing
  • City-by-city expansion with early-mover advantage

How Sponsorship Inventory Is Structured

Sponsorships are sold by city and page type. Inventory is intentionally limited to preserve quality and trust.

  • City Index Pages — high-visibility entry points
  • Category Pages — focused, intent-driven traffic
  • Educational Guides & FAQs — research-heavy reading
  • Directories — never mixed with advertising
