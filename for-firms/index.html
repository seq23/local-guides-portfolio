Advertising & Sponsorships

The Accident Guides offers clearly labeled, non-intrusive advertising placements alongside high-intent educational content. Sponsorships are fixed-price, transparent, and designed to scale city by city.

We are an independent educational publisher. Sponsorships do not influence editorial content.

How Sponsorship Placement Works

  • Sponsor placements appear in clearly labeled Advertising sections.
  • No lead selling, referral fees, or endorsements.
  • All placements are rendered dynamically via JSON — no manual page edits.
  • Editorial content remains unchanged regardless of sponsorship.

Where Your Firm Appears

Sponsorships are placed consistently across the site to maximize visibility while preserving a clean reading experience.

  • Phoenix City Index — Top, mid-page, and bottom placements
  • Phoenix Category Pages — Top, mid, and bottom category-exclusive slots
  • Phoenix Guides & FAQ — One multi-sponsor stack per page
  • Directories — Never mixed with advertising
