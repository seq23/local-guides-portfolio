Advertising & Sponsorships

The Accident Guides offers fixed, clearly disclosed sponsorship placements on an independent educational publishing platform.

The Accident Guides is designed for modern discovery. We publish structured, high-signal educational content optimized for both traditional search and AI-assisted discovery. As large language models increasingly influence how people research service providers, The Accident Guides is built to be a trusted reference surface in those environments.

We do not sell leads, referral traffic, or impressions. Sponsorships are limited, predictable placements that do not influence editorial content.

References to AI-assisted discovery describe general content architecture and visibility trends. The Accident Guides does not control, operate, or guarantee placement within third-party AI or search platforms.

How Sponsorships Work

  • All advertising is clearly labeled
  • Editorial content is created independently
  • No rotation, auctions, or performance guarantees
  • Inventory is intentionally limited to preserve trust and visibility

Premium Inventory: Home Page Hero

The visual banner at the top of the home page is the most prominent placement on the platform.

This placement is not rotated, not auctioned, and not sold à la carte.

It is included only with:

  • Total City Buyouts (for the active city)
  • Entire Website (National) Buyouts

Sponsorship Tiers & Pricing

Editorial Guide Presence — $990 / month

Shared sponsorship placement within educational guides and FAQs. Designed for firms seeking visibility without exclusivity.

  • Appears across multiple guide and FAQ pages
  • Clearly labeled as advertising
  • Non-exclusive

Category Ownership — $1,500 / month

Exclusive sponsorship of a specific accident category within a city (for example: Car Accidents or Truck Accidents).

  • Top, mid, and bottom placements on category pages
  • One sponsor per category
  • City-specific exclusivity

Citywide Authority — $1,500 / month

Prominent placement across a city’s main index page.

  • Multiple placements on the city index
  • Designed for local authority positioning
  • Does not include category exclusivity

Total City Buyout — $14,500 / month

Complete sponsorship control of a single city.

  • All city index placements
  • All category page placements
  • City-specific hero placement (when applicable)
  • One advertiser per city

Entire Website Buyout — $75,000 / month

A strategic, platform-wide exclusivity designed for a single national advertiser. This is not a scaled city package — it is ownership of the platform’s advertising layer.

  • Home page hero placement
  • All city pages (live and future)
  • All category pages and guides
  • Single advertiser across the entire platform

The Entire Website Buyout includes all current inventory and automatically extends to new cities as they launch, without renegotiation.

Right of First Refusal

Certain sponsorship tiers may include a Right of First Refusal (ROFR) for future inventory.

When applicable, ROFR provides the current sponsor the opportunity to secure newly launched cities, categories, or premium placements before they are made available to other advertisers.

ROFR terms are defined at the time of sponsorship agreement and are intended to preserve continuity and long-term strategic alignment.
