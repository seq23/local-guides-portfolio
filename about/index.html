About The Accident Guides

Independent, educational accident and injury resources — built for clarity, scale, and trust.

What We Are

The Accident Guides is an independent educational publishing platform that provides general information about accident types, recovery considerations, and commonly researched questions following injuries.

Our content is designed to help people understand topics they are already researching — not to replace professional legal or medical advice.

What We Are Not

We are not a law firm, medical provider, referral service, or lead broker. We do not sell leads, share personal data, or receive referral fees.

Any directories or listings published on this site are informational only and based on publicly available information.

How the Platform Works

The platform is organized around two core layers:

  • Educational content — guides, timelines, and checklists that address common accident-related questions.
  • Clearly labeled advertising — fixed placements that do not influence editorial content.

Editorial content is created independently. Advertising is always disclosed and never presented as a recommendation or endorsement.

City-Based Coverage

The Accident Guides is built to expand city by city, providing location-specific educational context where appropriate.

Phoenix, Arizona is currently live. Additional major U.S. cities are in development.

Advertising Transparency

Advertising placements are limited, fixed, and clearly labeled. We do not rotate ads or sell impressions.

National advertisers may claim a site-wide buyout, which includes ownership of all available premium placements across the platform, including:

  • Home page hero visual placement
  • City index pages
  • Category pages
  • Editorial guide placements

Site-wide placements are reserved for a single advertiser at a time and are clearly disclosed as advertising.

Educational content only. No legal advice. No medical advice. No guarantees or endorsements.
