Las Vegas FAQ

This FAQ explains what The Accident Guides is (and is not) for Las Vegas readers. This site provides educational information only.

Do I have to hire a lawyer?

No. Many people start with research and documentation. This site provides general educational information and optional directory access so you can understand topics at your own pace.

Is this legal advice?

No. The Accident Guides is an independent educational publisher. We do not provide legal advice or medical advice. For advice about your specific situation, consider contacting a licensed professional.

Do you recommend or endorse law firms?

No. Directory listings are informational only and based on publicly available information. Inclusion does not imply qualification, specialization, or endorsement.

How is this site funded?

Some pages may include clearly labeled advertising placements. Advertising does not change the educational content.

How much does a consultation cost?

Many firms offer free consultations, but policies vary. Confirm directly with any firm you contact.
