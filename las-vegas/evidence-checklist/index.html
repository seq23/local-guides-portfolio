Las Vegas Evidence Checklist

This page provides a general educational checklist of information people often document after an accident in Las Vegas, Nevada. It is not legal advice and does not replace professional guidance.

At the Scene (If Safe)

  • Photos and video of vehicles, damage, and the surrounding area
  • License plates, street signs, landmarks, and visible hazards
  • Weather, lighting, and roadway or property conditions
  • Contact and insurance information exchanged (where applicable)
  • Witness names and contact information (if willing)

Reports and Records

  • Any official report number or incident reference (if available)
  • Copies of reports when they become available
  • Medical visit summaries, discharge papers, and follow-up instructions
  • Receipts and invoices related to care, medications, or equipment

Ongoing Documentation

  • A simple timeline of symptoms, appointments, and recovery notes
  • Insurance communications (emails, letters, claim numbers)
  • Photos of injuries over time (if relevant)
  • Repair estimates or bills for property damage (if relevant)

Tips for Staying Organized

  • Create one folder (digital or physical) for everything related to the incident
  • Write down dates, names, and phone numbers after key calls
  • Keep originals and share copies when needed
