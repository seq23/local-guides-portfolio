What to Do After a Bicycle Accident

Bicycle accidents can occur on streets, bike lanes, and shared paths. Injuries may not always be immediately apparent, so caution is important. This page provides general educational guidance only.

  1. Move to a safe location if you can do so safely
  2. Call emergency services if you are injured
  3. Seek medical evaluation, even if symptoms seem minor
  4. Document the scene, bicycle damage, and surroundings
  5. Collect driver and witness contact information
  6. Keep copies of medical records and related expenses
