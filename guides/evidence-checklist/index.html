Accident Evidence Checklist

After an accident, people often focus on safety and medical care first. When possible, gathering basic documentation can help preserve details.

Scene Documentation

  • Photos or videos of the scene and surroundings
  • Images of vehicles, property, or hazards
  • Weather and lighting conditions

People Involved

  • Names and contact information
  • Insurance details when applicable
  • Witness contact information

Official Records

  • Police or incident reports
  • Reference or report numbers

Medical and Expense Records

  • Medical visit summaries
  • Receipts for related expenses
