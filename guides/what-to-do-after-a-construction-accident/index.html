What to Do After a Construction Accident

Construction accidents may affect workers, visitors, pedestrians, or nearby drivers. Safety and medical care are typically the first priorities.

  1. Call emergency services if injuries are serious
  2. Move to a safe location if possible
  3. Seek medical evaluation promptly
  4. Report the incident to a supervisor or site manager
  5. Document conditions, equipment, and surroundings if safe
  6. Keep copies of reports, records, and medical documents
