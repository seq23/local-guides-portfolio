What to Do After a Truck Accident

Truck accidents may involve commercial vehicles and additional complexity. Safety, medical care, and documentation are common priorities.

  1. Call emergency services if anyone is injured
  2. Move to a safe location if possible
  3. Seek prompt medical evaluation
  4. Document vehicles, cargo, and surroundings
  5. Collect contact and insurance information
  6. Preserve all related records
