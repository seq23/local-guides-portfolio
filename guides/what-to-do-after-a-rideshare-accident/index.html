What to Do After a Rideshare Accident

Rideshare accidents may involve drivers using app-based services, passengers, pedestrians, or other motorists. This page provides general educational guidance only.

  1. Ensure safety and call emergency services if needed
  2. Seek medical attention
  3. Document the scene and vehicles involved
  4. Collect contact information from all parties
  5. Save trip or ride details if available
  6. Keep copies of related records
