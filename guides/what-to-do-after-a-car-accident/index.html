What to Do After a Car Accident

Car accidents can be disorienting and stressful. Safety and medical care are typically the first priorities. This page outlines general steps people often consider afterward.

  1. Ensure your safety and call emergency services if needed
  2. Move vehicles out of traffic if it is safe to do so
  3. Seek medical evaluation, even for minor symptoms
  4. Document the scene, vehicles, and surroundings
  5. Exchange contact and insurance information
  6. Keep copies of reports, photos, and medical records
