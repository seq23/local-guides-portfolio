What to Do After a Motorcycle Accident

Motorcycle accidents often result in injuries due to limited physical protection. Medical attention and documentation are especially important considerations.

  1. Call emergency services if you are injured
  2. Move out of traffic if it is safe
  3. Seek medical care as soon as possible
  4. Document the scene and vehicle positions
  5. Exchange contact and insurance information
  6. Keep medical and repair records
