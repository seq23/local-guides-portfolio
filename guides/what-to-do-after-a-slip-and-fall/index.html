What to Do After a Slip and Fall

Slip and fall incidents can occur in stores, parking lots, workplaces, or other public and private spaces. Injuries are not always immediately apparent, so caution is important.

  1. Seek medical attention if you are injured or in pain
  2. Report the incident to the property owner or manager
  3. Document the hazard, location, and surrounding conditions
  4. Collect contact information from witnesses if available
  5. Request a copy of any incident report
  6. Keep medical records and related documentation
