What to Do After a Pedestrian Accident

Pedestrian accidents can happen suddenly and may involve serious injuries. Immediate medical attention and documentation are often priorities.

  1. Call 911 immediately if you are injured
  2. Move to a safe location if possible
  3. Seek medical evaluation as soon as possible
  4. Document the scene and the vehicle involved
  5. Collect contact information from witnesses
  6. Keep copies of medical and incident records
